Description:

Betta is a Google Chrome extension that aims to improve its users' focus by limiting time spent on distracting websites. A list of customized distracting websites to be blocked by the extension is accessed and edited by the user themself.

A gamified rewards system is used to encourage users by changing the health of virtual pet bettas based on user habits. The longer users stay on productive websites, the healthier their fish become. On the other hand, spending time on distracting websites will decrease the fish's health, eventually leading to its death.
